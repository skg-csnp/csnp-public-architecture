## Overview

CSNP provides Internal Developer Platform capabilities that enable engineering teams to safely build, deploy, and operate distributed services within standardized governance boundaries.

---

## Identity & Access Governance

Provides centralized authentication, authorization, and trust boundary enforcement.

Key capabilities:

* Single sign-on and identity federation
* Service-to-service authentication
* Access governance and policy enforcement
* Zero Trust access model

---

## Continuous Delivery Platform

Enables automated service build, test, and deployment pipelines.

Key capabilities:

* CI/CD pipeline orchestration
* Artifact and container image governance
* Environment promotion workflows
* Release governance and traceability

---

## GitOps Deployment Governance

Enforces declarative infrastructure and application deployment management.

Key capabilities:

* Git-based environment state management
* Deployment approval and promotion control
* Rollback and version tracking
* Multi-environment deployment standardization

---

## Secrets and Configuration Management

Provides secure management of application credentials and sensitive configuration.

Key capabilities:

* Centralized secret storage
* Dynamic secret injection
* Key rotation and lifecycle management
* Secure runtime configuration distribution

---

## Observability and Reliability

Provides system monitoring, tracing, and operational insight capabilities.

Key capabilities:

* Centralized logging
* Distributed tracing
* Service health monitoring
* Incident visibility and alerting

---

## Service Onboarding and Developer Experience

Provides templates and standards enabling consistent service development and deployment.

Key capabilities:

* Service template standards
* Deployment contract enforcement
* Development workflow standardization
* Platform guardrails and policy automation
